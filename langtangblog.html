Kyangjin Ri Trek

A 4-Day Adventure in Langtang with Friends

Langtang, with its stunning landscapes and serene valleys, offered an unforgettable trekking experience. Joined by my bachelor friends, we embarked on a 5-day journey filled with laughter, adventure, and breathtaking views. This blog captures the essence of our trip.

Day 1: Kathmandu to Bamboo

Our adventure began with a bike ride from Kathmandu to Syafrubesi, where we had breakfast at Nuwakot, Bidhur, and lunch at Syafrubesi. After safely parking our bikes, we trekked along the riverbank and reached Bamboo by 5 PM. We rested for the night at a cozy hotel.

Day 2:Bamboo to Langtang Valley

Our adventure began with a bike ride from Kathmandu to Syafrubesi, where we had breakfast at Nuwakot, Bidhur, and lunch at Syafrubesi. After safely parking our bikes, we trekked along the riverbank and reached Bamboo by 5 PM. We rested for the night at a cozy hotel.

Day 3:Langtang Valley to Kyangjin Gompa

Our adventure began with a bike ride from Kathmandu to Syafrubesi, where we had breakfast at Nuwakot, Bidhur, and lunch at Syafrubesi. After safely parking our bikes, we trekked along the riverbank and reached Bamboo by 5 PM. We rested for the night at a cozy hotel.

Day 4:Kyangjin Gompa to Lama Hotel

We had a long downhill march from Kyangjin Gompa to Lama Hotel where we rested for a night.

Day 5:Lama Hotel to Kathmandu

Our adventure with a hike downhill from Lama Hotel to Syafrubesi and then the bike ride from Syafrubesi to Kathmandu, where we had breakfast at Lama Hotel, and lunch at Syafrubesi. We reached kathmandu late night and had our dinner at airport side and went home.
